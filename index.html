TOP 3 RICE

In this page I share my top 3 rice types based on my personal preference, taste and cooking time:
  1. Jasmine rice
  2. Basmati rice
  3. Long Grain White rice

In my opinion jasmin rice is the best because of the short cooking time (20 min) and the aroma. Basmati rice is my second favorite, about the same cooking time as jasin rice and it has a nice texture. Long grain white rice is my third favorite, it takes a bit longer to cook (25 min) and has a more neutral flavor.

Jasmin Rice

Ingedients

  1. 1 cup of jasmin rice
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. 1/2 tsp of salt or salt to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring the water with the salt to a boil.
  2. Add the rice to the boiling water, make sure all the rice is under water.
  3. Immediately after you added the rice to the boiling water, reduce the fire to low.
  4. Cover the pot with a lid and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. After 15 minutes of cooking, turn off the heat and let it sit for another 5 minutes.
  6. After the 5 minutes passed, remove the lid and fluff the rice with a fork.
